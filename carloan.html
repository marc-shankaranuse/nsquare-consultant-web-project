Car Loan

A car loan is a secured loan offered to individuals or businesses to purchase a new or used car. The car itself serves as collateral, making it easier to avail the loan. Car loans are available with competitive interest rates and flexible repayment options.

Key Features of Car Loans

  • Loan Amount: Covers up to 90–100% of the car's on-road price.
  • Interest Rate: Typically ranges between 7% and 13%, depending on credit profile and lender policies.
  • Loan Tenure: Usually between 1 to 7 years.
  • Repayment: Flexible EMI options to suit your budget.
  • Processing Speed: Quick approval, often within hours.
Common Uses for Car Loans
  • New Car Purchase: Financing new cars for personal or commercial use.
  • Used Car Purchase: Specific loans for purchasing pre-owned vehicles.
  • Fleet Expansion: Adding vehicles for business purposes like logistics or taxis.
  • Loan for Electric Vehicles: Supporting the purchase of eco-friendly electric cars.
Eligibility Criteria
  • Income Proof: Regular and sufficient income to repay the loan.
  • Credit Score: A score above 650 is usually required for approval.
  • Employment: Stable employment or business with consistent income history.
  • Age: Typically between 21 and 65 years.
Key Advantages of Car Loans
  • Affordable Interest Rates: Lower due to the secured nature of the loan.
  • Flexible Tenures: EMIs can be tailored to match your financial capacity.
  • Quick Approvals: Ideal for urgent car purchases.
  • No Need for Additional Collateral: The car itself serves as security.
Points to Consider
  • Interest Rates: Check for competitive rates to save on total repayment.
  • Processing Fees: Some lenders charge processing or prepayment fees.
  • Impact on Credit Score: Non-payment or late payments can affect your score.
  • Car Insurance: Ensure the car is insured as required by lenders.
