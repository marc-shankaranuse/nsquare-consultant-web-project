Home Loan

A home loan, also known as a housing loan or mortgage, is a financial product offered by banks or other lending institutions that enables individuals to purchase a home. Hereâ€™s a brief overview:

Key Features of Home Loans

  • Loan Amount: Based on the value of the property, income, credit history, and repayment capacity.
  • Interest Rate: Fixed or floating rates. Floating rates fluctuate with market conditions, while fixed rates remain constant.
  • Loan Tenure: Typically ranges from 5 to 30 years.
  • Equated Monthly Installments (EMIs): Monthly repayments that include principal and interest components.
  • Down Payment: Usually 10-20% of the property cost.
Types of Home Loans
  • Home Purchase Loan: For purchasing a new or resale property.
  • Home Construction Loan: For constructing a house on a plot of land.
  • Home Improvement Loan: For renovating or repairing an existing property.
  • Home Extension Loan: For extending or expanding an existing home.
  • Balance Transfer Loan: Transferring the outstanding balance to another lender for lower interest rates.
Eligibility Criteria
  • Income and Employment Stability
  • Credit Score: Usually 650 or above.
  • Age: Typically between 21 and 65 years.
  • Property Documents: Clear title and legal documentation of the property.
Tax Benefits
  • Interest Deduction: Up to Rs. 2 lakh per year on interest payments under Section 24.
  • Principal Deduction: Up to Rs. 1.5 lakh under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act.
