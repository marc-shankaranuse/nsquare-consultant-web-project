Personal Loan

A personal loan is an unsecured loan that individuals can use for various personal needs, such as medical emergencies, weddings, education, home renovations, or debt consolidation. Since it's unsecured, no collateral is required, making it a flexible financing option.

Key Features of Personal Loans

  • Loan Amount:Generally ranges from a few thousand to several lakhs, depending on income and creditworthiness
  • Interest Rate:Often higher than secured loans, as it’s unsecured. Rates can vary based on credit score, lender, and borrower profile.
  • Loan Tenure: Typically between 1 to 5 years, though some lenders may offer a tenure of up to 7 years.
  • Equated Monthly Installments (EMIs): Monthly installments include both principal and interest, and you can calculate them with an EMI calculator.
  • Quick Approval: Personal loans often have faster approval processes, with some lenders offering same-day or 24-hour disbursal.
Different Uses for Personal Loans
  • Medical Expense
  • Education or Skill Development
  • Home Improvement
  • Debt Consolidation
  • Travel or Vacation Expenses
Eligibility Criteria
  • Income: Regular income with proof
  • Credit Score: Generally 650 or higher, with better rates available for higher scores.
  • Age: Typically between 21 and 65 years.
  • Employment: Employed or self-employed individuals with consistent income history.
Key Advantages
  • No Collateral Needed: Reduces the risk of asset loss.
  • Flexible Use: You can use the loan amount for any personal need.
  • Quick Processing: Suitable for urgent needs.
Points to Consider
  • Higher Interest Rates: Due to the unsecured nature of the loan.
  • Impact on Credit Score: Non-payment or late payments can impact credit score.
  • Prepayment Penalties: Some lenders charge fees if you wish to repay the loan early.
