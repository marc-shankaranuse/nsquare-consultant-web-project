Buisness Loan

A business loan is a financial product designed to meet the funding requirements of businesses. It helps organizations cover operational expenses, expansion, inventory purchase, or working capital needs. Business loans are offered as secured or unsecured options depending on the amount and borrower profile.

Key Features of Buisness Loans

  • Loan Amount:Ranges from a few lakhs to several crores, depending on the scale of the business and revenue.
  • Interest Rate:Competitive rates, starting from 8% to 20%, influenced by creditworthiness, business performance, and lender policies.
  • Loan Tenure:Typically ranges from 1 to 7 years for most loans, with some lenders offering extended periods for larger amounts.
  • Flexible Repayment Options:Includes options like bullet repayment, EMIs, or customized repayment schedules.
  • Collateral Options:Can be collateral-free (under certain government schemes like CGTMSE) or secured by assets like property, equipment, or inventory..
Different Uses for Buisness Loans
  • Business Expansion: Scaling operations or entering new markets.
  • Inventory Purchase: Stocking up on raw materials or finished goods.
  • Working Capital: Addressing short-term financial gaps.
  • Equipment Purchase: Acquiring machinery or vehicles for business use.
  • Emergency Funding: Managing unforeseen expenses.
Eligibility Criteria
  • Business Age: Must have been operational for at least 2-3 years.
  • Revenue Stability: Proof of steady revenue through financial statements.
  • Credit Score: Typically 700 or above for the best rates.
  • Business Registration: Proper documentation including tax filings and licenses.
Key Advantages
  • High Loan Amounts: Suitable for small, medium, and large enterprises.
  • Collateral-Free Options: Ideal for small businesses under government schemes.
  • Customized Repayment Plans: Tailored to fit business cash flow.
  • Quick Approvals: Especially for pre-approved customers.
