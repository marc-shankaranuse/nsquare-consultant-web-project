Health Insurance

Health insurance is a type of coverage that pays for medical and surgical expenses incurred by the insured. It helps cover costs for doctor visits, hospital stays, medications, and surgeries. In India, there are various types of health insurance policies, including:

Types of Health Insurance
  • Individual Health Insurance:Covers a single person.
  • Family Floater:Provides coverage for the entire family under one policy.
  • Critical Illness Insurance: Pays a lump sum if diagnosed with specific critical illnesses.
  • Senior Citizen Health Insurance:Designed for people over 60. .
  • Group Health Insurance: Often provided by employers to their employees.
Benefits of Health Insurance

health insurance include financial protection against high medical expenses, access to quality healthcare, and tax benefits under Section 80D of the Income Tax Act.
